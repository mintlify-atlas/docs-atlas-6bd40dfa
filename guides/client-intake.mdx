## Overview

The client intake process captures essential information about households receiving food deliveries. This guide walks you through creating a complete client profile from start to finish.

## Starting a New Client Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Clients page">
    Click on **Clients** in the main navigation menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new profile">
    Click the **Add Client** or **New Profile** button to open a blank client form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The form will be in edit mode automatically for new profiles. All required fields must be completed before you can save.
</Note>

## Required Information

### Basic Information

Complete these essential fields first:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter client name">
    - **First Name** - Client's legal first name (required)
    - **Last Name** - Client's legal last name (required)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter contact information">
    - **Phone** - Primary contact number (required, minimum 10 digits)
    - **Alternative Phone** - Secondary contact number (optional)
    - **Email** - Email address (optional, but must be valid format if provided)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter address information">
    - **Address** - Street address including apartment/unit number (required)
    - **Address 2** - Additional address details (optional)
    - **City** - City name (required)
    - **State** - Must be DC, MD, or VA (required)
    - **ZIP Code** - 5-digit postal code (required)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The system will automatically validate the address and calculate the ward. Make sure to select a valid address from Google Places suggestions when typing.
</Warning>

### Household Composition

Enter the number of people in the household:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter household members">
    - **Adults** - Number of adults (18-64 years)
    - **Seniors** - Number of seniors (65+ years)
    - **Children** - Number of children (under 18 years)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Identify head of household">
    - **Head of Household** - Select either "Adult" or "Senior"
    - Must match the household composition (can't select "Senior" if seniors = 0)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
At least one adult or senior is required. The total household size is automatically calculated.
</Note>

### Demographics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter demographic information">
    - **Date of Birth** - Client's date of birth (format: MM/DD/YYYY)
    - **Gender** - Select from available options
    - **Ethnicity** - Select from available options (required)
    - **Language** - Primary language spoken (required)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delivery Schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set delivery dates">
    - **Start Date** - First delivery date (format: MM/DD/YYYY, required)
    - **End Date** - Last scheduled delivery date (format: MM/DD/YYYY, required)
    - **Recurrence** - Select delivery frequency: None, Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly, or Custom (required)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Referral Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select referral source">
    - **Referral Entity** - Choose the organization or case worker who referred the client (required)
    - **Referred Date** - Date client was referred (optional)
    - **TEFAP Cert** - TEFAP certification date if applicable (optional, cannot be in the future)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you need to add a new case worker or referral agency, click the **Manage Case Workers** button to add them before completing the intake.
</Tip>

## Optional Information

### Delivery Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add delivery details">
    Enter specific instructions for drivers such as:
    - Gate codes or building access information
    - Preferred delivery location
    - Special handling instructions
    - Contact preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Dietary Restrictions

Select all applicable dietary needs:

- Low Sugar (Diabetes Friendly)
- Kidney Friendly
- Vegan
- Vegetarian
- Halal
- Microwave Only
- Soft Food
- Low Sodium
- No Cooking Equipment
- Heart Friendly
- Food Allergies (specify in the text field)
- Other (specify in the text field)

### Health Information

Document any health conditions:

**Physical Ailments:**
- Diabetes
- Hypertension
- Heart Disease
- Kidney Disease
- Cancer
- Other (specify)

**Physical Disabilities:**
- Check if applicable and specify

**Mental Health Conditions:**
- Check if applicable and specify

### Additional Fields

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete remaining sections">
    - **Life Challenges** - Current challenges the client is facing
    - **Lifestyle Goals** - Client's goals and aspirations
    - **Notes** - Any additional important information
    - **Tags** - Add relevant tags for categorization
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Saving the Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review all information">
    Double-check that all required fields are completed and accurate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the profile">
    Click the **Save** button at the top of the form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Address duplicate check">
    The system will automatically check for duplicate clients. If a match is found:
    - Review the duplicate warning message
    - Verify this is truly a new client
    - If it's actually a duplicate, cancel and search for the existing profile instead
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm successful save">
    A confirmation message will appear, and a unique 12-digit Client ID will be generated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The system prevents duplicate clients based on matching name and address. If you receive a duplicate error, search for the existing client profile before proceeding.
</Warning>

## After Creating the Profile

Once saved, you can:

- **Schedule Deliveries** - Click "Add Delivery" to create delivery events
- **View Delivery History** - See past and upcoming deliveries
- **Edit Information** - Click the Edit button to update client details
- **Add Tags** - Categorize the client for reporting and filtering

## Validation Rules

<Warning>
The following will prevent saving:
</Warning>

- Missing required fields (First Name, Last Name, Address, City, State, ZIP, Phone, Gender, Ethnicity, Language)
- Invalid email format
- Invalid phone number format (must have at least 10 digits)
- State not DC, MD, or VA
- TEFAP cert date in the future
- No adults or seniors in household
- Head of household doesn't match household composition
- Missing referral entity
- Duplicate client (same name and address)

## Phone Number Formats

Accepted phone number formats:
- (123) 456-7890
- 123-456-7890
- 123.456.7890
- 123 456 7890
- 1234567890
- +1 123-456-7890

<Tip>
The system will automatically format and validate phone numbers as you type.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify information with the client">
    Confirm all details directly with the client or referral source when possible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Be thorough with delivery instructions">
    Detailed delivery instructions help drivers complete deliveries successfully.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Document dietary restrictions carefully">
    Accurate dietary information ensures clients receive appropriate food.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use tags consistently">
    Apply tags systematically to make searching and reporting more effective.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Keep notes professional">
    Document relevant information while maintaining client dignity and privacy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Existing Profiles

To update a client profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the client">
    Search for and open the client's profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter edit mode">
    Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) at the top of the profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update the necessary fields. The form follows the same validation rules as new profiles.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to update the profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Changes to certain fields (notes, delivery instructions, life challenges, lifestyle goals) are timestamped automatically to maintain an audit trail.
</Note>
