## Overview

The delivery management system allows you to schedule deliveries, assign drivers, and track delivery status. This guide covers the complete delivery workflow from scheduling to completion.

## Accessing the Calendar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Calendar">
    Click **Calendar** in the main navigation menu to open the delivery calendar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your view">
    Select between:
    - **Day View** - Detailed view of a single day's deliveries
    - **Month View** - Overview of all deliveries across the month
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a New Delivery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Delivery dialog">
    Click the **Add Delivery** button (+ icon) in the calendar header.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the client">
    - Start typing the client's name in the search field
    - Select the correct client from the dropdown list
    - The system will pre-fill delivery preferences from the client profile
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the delivery date">
    Select the date for the delivery. The system will show:
    - Current delivery capacity for that day
    - Warning if the day is at or over capacity
    - Daily limit based on configured constraints
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set recurrence pattern">
    Choose how often the delivery should repeat:
    - **None** - One-time delivery only
    - **Weekly** - Repeats every week
    - **Bi-Weekly** - Repeats every two weeks
    - **Monthly** - Repeats monthly
    - **Custom** - Select specific dates from a calendar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set end date (for recurring deliveries)">
    If you selected Weekly, Bi-Weekly, or Monthly:
    - Enter the **Repeats End Date** when the recurring deliveries should stop
    - The system will automatically calculate all delivery dates in the series
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    - Check the delivery summary
    - Verify the number of deliveries being created
    - Click **Add Delivery** to confirm
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The system automatically checks for duplicate deliveries. If the client already has a delivery scheduled on a particular date, that date will be skipped.
</Note>

## Delivery Recurrence Options

### One-Time Delivery
Schedules a single delivery on the selected date.

### Weekly Delivery
- Repeats every 7 days from the start date
- Continues until the end date
- Example: Every Monday from 1/1/2024 to 3/31/2024

### Bi-Weekly Delivery
- Repeats every 14 days from the start date
- Useful for clients receiving deliveries every other week
- Example: Every other Tuesday from 1/2/2024 to 6/30/2024

### Monthly Delivery
- Repeats on the same day of the month
- Continues until the end date
- Example: 15th of each month from 1/15/2024 to 12/15/2024

### Custom Dates
- Select specific dates from a calendar
- Useful for irregular schedules
- Each date is individually selected

<Warning>
Recurring deliveries create multiple delivery events. Make sure the end date is correct before confirming, as this will create all delivery records at once.
</Warning>

## Understanding Delivery Capacity

The system tracks daily delivery limits:

- Each day has a configured maximum number of deliveries
- The calendar shows current capacity vs. limit
- Color-coded warnings indicate when approaching or exceeding capacity:
  - **Green** - Within capacity
  - **Yellow** - Near capacity
  - **Red** - Over capacity

<Tip>
You can still schedule deliveries when over capacity, but it's recommended to stay within limits for operational efficiency.
</Tip>

## Viewing Deliveries

### Day View

The Day View displays:
- All deliveries scheduled for the selected day
- Client names and addresses
- Assigned drivers
- Delivery times
- Clusters and routes
- Delivery instructions
- Dietary restrictions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate between days">
    - Click **Previous** to go to the previous day
    - Click **Next** to go to the next day
    - Click **Today** to return to today's date
  </Step>

  <Step title="View delivery details">
    Click on any delivery card to see:
    - Full client information
    - Complete address
    - Phone number
    - Delivery notes
    - Dietary restrictions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Month View

The Month View shows:
- Number of deliveries per day
- Capacity indicators for each day
- Color-coded days based on capacity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate between months">
    - Click **Previous** to go to the previous month
    - Click **Next** to go to the next month
    - Click **Today** to return to the current month
  </Step>

  <Step title="View a specific day">
    Click on any date in the month view to switch to Day View for that date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Deliveries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the delivery">
    Find the delivery in either Day or Month view.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open delivery details">
    Click on the delivery to view its details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make changes">
    You can update:
    - Delivery date
    - Assigned driver
    - Delivery time
    - Notes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save** to update the delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Changes to a single delivery in a recurring series will only affect that specific delivery, not the entire series.
</Warning>

## Deleting Deliveries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the delivery">
    Click on the delivery you want to delete.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Click the **Delete** button and confirm the action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Deleting a delivery is permanent. The system will ask for confirmation before proceeding.
</Note>

## Managing Recurring Deliveries

### Viewing Recurring Series

Deliveries that are part of a recurring series will show:
- Recurrence pattern (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)
- Series start date
- Series end date

### Editing Recurring Series

When editing a recurring delivery:
- Changes apply only to the selected instance
- To modify the entire series, you must delete and recreate it

### Canceling Future Deliveries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the series">
    Find any delivery in the recurring series.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete future instances">
    Manually delete each future delivery you want to cancel, or delete all remaining deliveries by updating the client's end date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delivery Status

Track delivery completion:

- **Scheduled** - Delivery is planned but not yet completed
- **Completed** - Delivery has been made
- **Past Due** - Delivery date has passed

<Note>
Completed deliveries appear in the client's delivery history on their profile page.
</Note>

## Capacity Management

### Viewing Daily Limits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the limits editor">
    In Month view, click the **Edit Limits** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review current limits">
    See the maximum deliveries allowed for each day of the week.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Understanding Limit Warnings

The system provides visual indicators:
- Days with available capacity appear normal
- Days approaching capacity show warnings
- Days over capacity are highlighted in red

<Tip>
Plan deliveries in advance to distribute load evenly across available days.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schedule deliveries in advance">
    Create delivery schedules at least one week ahead to allow for route planning.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use recurring deliveries for regular clients">
    Set up weekly or bi-weekly recurring deliveries for clients with consistent needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor capacity daily">
    Check the calendar regularly to ensure balanced delivery loads.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update delivery information promptly">
    If a client's address or delivery instructions change, update the profile immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review delivery history">
    Use the client profile to view past deliveries and ensure service continuity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

**"This date is over capacity"**
- Consider scheduling for a different day
- Check if the delivery is urgent or can be moved
- Verify that daily limits are set correctly

**"Client already has a delivery on this date"**
- The client is already scheduled for that day
- Check the calendar to see the existing delivery
- Choose a different date or edit the existing delivery

**Delivery not appearing in calendar**
- Refresh the page
- Check that you're viewing the correct date range
- Verify the delivery was saved successfully

<Warning>
If you experience persistent issues with deliveries not saving or appearing, contact your system administrator.
</Warning>
